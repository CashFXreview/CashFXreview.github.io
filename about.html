About SteveyMcGuy

Who are we?

SteveyMcGuy is a passionate group of people that love games and love making games. We hope to challenge and transform the market with better, higher quality games for everyone.

What do we do?

We make games. Small indie games for now, but with a passion. We don't settle for basic run-of-the-mill games and saturate them with ads or charge exorbitant prices. We enjoy making special hand crafted games that are enjoyable to play.
